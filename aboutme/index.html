About me (cybersecurity)#

In 2018, I got into the world of cybersecurity thanks to enrolling in MPEI university, or rather, the CTF movement held at a university. Now I am a member of the “Unicorn Team” (university CTF team). I like to solve osint, cryptography, network, sometimes web, forensics. I worked for 2 years at Kaspersky Lab as a web threat analyst. More than once I won or became a prize-winner in various competitions related to cybersecurity. I also attend annual conferences in this area. I was a speaker at events at Positive Technologies, at Bank of Russia, at MPEI university, and various schools on information or cyber security for students. From time to time I am the organizer of various competitions and other events on cybersecurity and osint. Among the goals for the future, of course, I put development and career growth in the field of cybersecurity.

My posts will be about…#

I will write exclusively about topics related to cybersecurity (osint, web threats, networks, auditing, cyber news, phishing): I will show my researches, interesting finds on the web, useful tools, explore different malware samples, CTF tasks (which I solved and which I wrote myself), the competitions in which I participate. Due to the heavy workload (work, study, projects with different companies), I will publish posts infrequently, but I will try at least once a month.
